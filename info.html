Information


About the server

The Factions CMP (Creative Multi-player) community is a vibrant and welcoming group of Minecraft players brought together by a shared passion for creativity and collaborative building. Centered around the Factions CMP Minecraft server, this community thrives on imagination, teamwork, and artistic expression. Unlike traditional Minecraft factions servers that often emphasize competition or survival, Factions CMP is dedicated primarily to creative construction. The server invites players to either join existing factions or form their own, each typically unified by a distinct architectural style or creative theme.
Within this flexible and open environment, players are encouraged to explore different aesthetics and techniques. It's common for members to participate in multiple factions simultaneously, allowing them to collaborate with a variety of builders and experiment with a range of building styles. This approach keeps the experience fresh and engaging, preventing creative stagnation and offering endless opportunities for inspiration. Whether you're into medieval castles, futuristic cities, organic landscapes, or anything in between, the Factions CMP community provides a supportive space where your ideas can come to life alongside others who share your creative spirit.

How to join

To join the Factions CMP Minecraft community, you must first complete an application form, which can be found on the Apply page. This form will ask for your Minecraft username and basic contact information. You will also be required to answer several questions about your previous experiences on creative Minecraft servers. Once submitted, your application will be reviewed by an administrator. Please note that this review process may take between one to three weeks. After the review, an administrator will contact you to inform you of the outcome. If your application is successful, your Minecraft username will be confirmed, and you will receive an invitation to join the discord server.

How to access the minecraft server

Once you have received confirmation of your acceptance into the Minecraft server and have joined the official Discord server, you will be granted access to the server IP address. This will allow you to connect to the Factions CMP Minecraft server.
To join the server, please follow these steps:

  • Open the Minecraft launcher.
  • Create a new installation using version 1.20.1.
  • Launch the game using this installation.
  • Navigate to the Multiplayer section.
  • Click the “Add Server” button.
  • In the Server Name field, enter: Factions CMP.
  • In the Server Address field, enter the IP address provided to you on the Discord server.
  • Click “Done” to save the server details.
  • Select the newly added server and click “Join Server” to connect.

If you encounter any issues during setup, feel free to reach out to an administrator through the Discord server for assistance.
